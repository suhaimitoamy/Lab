# Private Trading Course: XAUUSD Scalping & Smart Money Concepts

Selamat datang di materi pembelajaran trading eksklusif! Repositori ini berisi kurikulum terstruktur untuk menguasai trading XAUUSD (Gold) menggunakan kombinasi pembacaan Market Structure dan Smart Money Concepts (SMC).

Materi telah disusun dari tingkat dasar hingga ahli agar Anda dapat membangun fondasi yang kuat sebelum masuk ke eksekusi "Sniper Entry".

## Daftar Isi Kurikulum

### Modul 1: Market Structure (Struktur Pasar)
* **[Level 1: Beginner - Dasar-Dasar Market Structure](market_structure/01_beginner.md)**
  * Memahami Uptrend, Downtrend, Sideways.
  * Support & Resistance Dasar.
  * Konsep Break and Retest (RBS & SBR).
* **[Level 2: Intermediate - Anatomi Tren & Perubahan Karakter](market_structure/02_intermediate.md)**
  * Higher High, Higher Low, Lower High, Lower Low.
  * Break of Structure (BoS).
  * Change of Character (ChoCh).
* **[Level 3: Advanced - Konsep Smart Money (SMC)](market_structure/03_advanced.md)**
  * Order Block (OB).
  * Imbalance / Fair Value Gap (FVG).
  * Liquidity (Likuiditas) dan Inducement.
* **[Level 4: Expert - MTFA, Sniper Entry, & Manajemen Risiko](market_structure/04_expert.md)**
  * Multi-Timeframe Analysis (MTFA).
  * Market Structure Shift (MSS) / Konfirmasi LTF.
  * Blueprint Sniper Entry.
  * Manajemen Risiko dan Psikologi Trading.
